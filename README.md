# Prism Insurance Data Analysis Project

## Executive Summary
This project analyzes the financial and operational performance of Prism Insurance Pvt. Ltd. The interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive view of policy distributions, claim resolutions, and demographic trends to help stakeholders understand portfolio risks and revenue drivers.

## Key Insights & Business Findings

* **Top Performing Policy Types:** Travel insurance generates the highest premium amount for the business at 2.5M[cite: 3]. Health and Auto insurance follow with 1.2M and 1.0M respectively[cite: 3]. Home insurance generates the lowest premium amount at 0.6M[cite: 3].
* **Financial Overview:** The portfolio handles a total coverage amount of 600.55M[cite: 3]. This brings in 5.98M in premium amounts against 16.91M in total claim amounts[cite: 3].
* **Demographic Claim Trends:** Policyholders in the "Adults" age group account for the highest claim amount at 7.5M[cite: 3]. Senior Citizens follow closely at 6.4M, while Young Adults account for 3.0M[cite: 3].
* **Customer Base:** The user base has a nearly equal gender split, consisting of 5,003 males and 5,001 females[cite: 3].
* **Policy Retention:** A majority of the policies are currently Inactive, comprising 52.67% (5.27K) of the total[cite: 3]. Active policies make up the remaining 47.33% (4.74K)[cite: 3].
* **Claim Resolutions:** Regarding claim status, the highest number of claims are Rejected at 4.4K[cite: 3]. Settled claims total 3.4K, and 2.3K claims are currently Pending[cite: 3].

## Technical Details
* **Tool Used:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Source:** CSV dataset (`InsuranceData.csv`) containing policy and claim-level data.
* **Key Visualizations:** Donut charts for policy status tracking, line charts for demographic claim trends, and detailed matrix tables for individual policy inspection.
